## The DDoS Protection Actually Works

This is worth spending a sentence on because it's a differentiator. Most budget hosts either skip DDoS protection or slap a nominal "protection layer" on their marketing page that collapses under a real attack. ExtraVM runs a two-layer system: high-capacity upstream filtering through partners like Global Secure Layer and Datapacket at most locations, plus their own in-house eBPF/XDP filters at the network edge. The in-house layer processes traffic at the Linux kernel level before it reaches user space — which means filtering happens without noticeable latency.

For Minecraft server operators and game hosting customers in particular, this is genuinely useful. Game servers get targeted constantly. Trustpilot reviewers have described their servers surviving DDoS attacks — including methods specifically designed to bypass lesser protections — without any downtime or lag. That's the protection doing its job without anyone noticing.
